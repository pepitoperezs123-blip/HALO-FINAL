About Halocompounds

Halocompounds supplies high-purity peptide reference standards to the research community. We exist to make well-characterized, properly documented research materials easy for laboratories to source — with transparent specifications and a strict research-use-only standard.

What we do

We provide lyophilized peptide reference materials with analytical characterization (HPLC, mass spectrometry) and per-lot certificates of analysis available on request. Every listing publishes its CAS number, molecular formula, mass, purity, physical form, and storage requirements so researchers know exactly what they are receiving.

Our standard

We sell exclusively for laboratory research use. Our products are not drugs, supplements, foods, or cosmetics, and are not for human or veterinary use. We require an intended-use acknowledgment from every buyer and decline orders that fall outside legitimate research.

Documentation first

Reproducible research depends on knowing your materials. That is why characterization data and COAs sit at the center of how we operate.

Quality commitments

  • Purity verified by HPLC and reported on every product.
  • Per-lot certificate of analysis available on request.
  • Stability-conscious packaging and clear handling guidance.
  • Responsive support for researchers and procurement teams.
